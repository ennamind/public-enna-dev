According to a report by Mintel, the UK dental market is projected to reach a value of £9.62 billion in 20221. The dental market in the UK continues to be fragmented with 12,085 registered dental companies and 14,340 dental practices in total2. Between 2016 and 2019, UK expenditure on dental care rose by just over 7% – from £9.35 billion to £10.05 billion3. The United Kingdom Dental Devices Market is expected to register a CAGR of nearly 6.1% during the forecast period (2022-2027)4.  As for trends, 46% of UK adults have visited an NHS dentist in the last 12 months1. 57% of people who have visited a dentist in the last five years received a general examination or check-up1. 70% of Brits use self-payment for their dental treatments1.
